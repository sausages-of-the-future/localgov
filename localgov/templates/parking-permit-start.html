Parking permits

Resident parking permits allow you to park in resident bays and resident shared use bays in the Controlled Parking Zone in which you live:

  • each permit is vehicle specific and cannot be used on any other vehicle
  • permits are restricted to one permit per vehicle
  • the cost of a parking permit depends on the emissions the vehicle produces
